🏛️ Three Levels of Government

Did you know Canada has three levels of government, and each one collects money (called revenue) and spends it on different things? Taxes are the biggest way governments collect money - and they use it to pay for schools, hospitals, roads, police, and much more!

Let's explore how each level works, where the money comes from, and where it all goes. Ready? Let's dive in! 🚀

🏘️

Municipal / Regional

Your city or region - handles local stuff like water, roads, garbage, and police.

🏫

Provincial

Ontario's government - pays for schools, hospitals, and highways.

🇨🇦

Federal

The whole country - handles defence, EI, Indigenous services, and transfers to provinces.

💸 How Money Flows Between Governments

Money doesn't just stay in one place - the federal government sends billions to provinces, and provinces send money to municipalities too!
